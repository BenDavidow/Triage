This visual breaks down the impact of reducing consumption of different animal foods. The sliders begin at an average American's consumption level of each food. Adjust the amount consumed of different foods and the corresponding amount of annual suffering caused will display on the right. If, for example, you drag eggs half way down, that equals a person splitting his or her egg consumption in half, and you'll see the color that represents eggs (purple) adjust on the right. By hovering over the impact bars, you can see the impact of each food in more detail.
